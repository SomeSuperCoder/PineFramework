## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Go To Date
The chart SHALL provide a "Go to Date" feature allowing users to jump to a specific date/time in the data.

#### Scenario: Go To Date Dialog
- **WHEN** the user activates "Go to Date"
- **THEN** a date picker SHALL be displayed

#### Scenario: Date Navigation
- **WHEN** the user selects a date/time
- **THEN** the chart SHALL scroll to that date
